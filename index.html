About Us

Whether it's a simple cup of brewed loose leaf tea or a refreshing cup of creamy milk tea, we strive to bring you the highest quality and the freshest ingredients. Our variety of teas are sourced from the mountains of China, Taiwan, and Japan, while our dairy is shipped from local farms.

Chewy tapioca balls, also known as "pearls" or "boba," originated in the city of Taichung, Taiwan, but have gained global popularity as a topping in milk teas and other sweet beverages. We cook fresh batches of tapioca balls every morning. Try it in your drink today!
